## Slide 2: Overview

### 5 Stages of the Customer Journey

| Stage | Name | Duration | Focus |
|-------|------|---------|-------|
| 1 | **Registration** | < 5 min | Sign up, verification |
| 2 | **Onboarding** | 1-2 days | Profile, labs, Twin init |
| 3 | **Enriching** | 2-4 weeks | Daily tracking, data collection |
| 4 | **Recommendations** | Ongoing | AI-generated interventions |
| 5 | **Optimization** | 6-12 months | Longevity path |

---

## Slide 3: Stage 1 - Registration

### Getting Users Onboard

```
Landing Page → Sign Up → Email Verify → Account Active
```

**Data Collected:**
- Email
- Name
- Password

**Channels:** Web, Mobile App

**Target Metrics:**
- Sign-up rate: 30%
- Time to complete: < 5 minutes

---

## Slide 4: Stage 2 - Onboarding

### Building the Profile

```
Health Profile → Health Quiz → Wearable Connect → Blood Test → Digital Twin Init
```

**Data Collected:**
- Demographics (age, gender, weight, height)
- Medical history
- Lifestyle factors
- Wearable data (HRV, sleep, activity)
- Blood biomarkers

**Digital Twin Initialization:**
- 24-48 hours processing time
- Multi-omics integration

---

## Slide 5: Stage 3 - Enriching

### Continuous Data Collection

**Daily:**
- Morning mood & sleep quality
- Dietary intake
- Activity tracking

**Weekly:**
- Progress check-in
- Weight & energy levels

**Wearable Auto-sync:**
- HRV trends
- Sleep stages
- Activity patterns
